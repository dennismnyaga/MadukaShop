About Maduka Online

An exclusive connection to the Kenyan market for better choices.
Who we are
Madukaonline is a platform inspired by digitalization whose main goal is to bring all forms of business together.
We make this possible by providing businesses with an opportunity to make a presence online by opening their shop online and making it possible for buyers to locate them online.
Our mission
